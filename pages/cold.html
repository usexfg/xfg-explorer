Certificate Of Ledger Deposits

Fuego's DeFi Banking Suite & Asset DAO

The purpose of COLD Banking Suite is to create an in-house Defi money market for XFG deposit yield.

Allowing XFG to function as a yield-bearing asset, which not only creates additional network utility ( holders earn tokens (currency, NFTs, etc) as interest on XFG deposit yield- exchangeable in a DeFi environment; without ever trading XFG) but more importantly, continues preserving the original hard supply and sound money principals of Fango.

Details on all off-chain tokens to be offered thru COLD will be listed below.

C0LD DAO Governance

Proposals: Anyone may propose a vote so long as they a) hold the min amount required for type of proposal (monetary or project) -&- b) make some kind of improvement to the voting interface from previous voting session.

    1st Governance session (3/20/21) : Holding a minimum of 0.1 $COLD is now required for Monetary Policy while holding ANY amount of $COLD allows users to vote on project governance decisions.
